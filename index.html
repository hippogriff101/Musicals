Matilda the Musical

Matilda the Musical is based on the hit book by Roald Dahl. The musical adaptation combines my love for books with theater.

It is shown at the Cambridge Theatre in the London West End.

The Lion King

I went to see The Lion King in London West End for my birthday in 2024 and my gosh it was phenomenal. It combines my love / nostalgia for the orginal film with the wonders of the theater world.

The Lion King is shown at the Lyceum Theatre in London West End.

Greese the Musical

Greese the Musical was shown in the London West End for a short duration of time in 2023 and I was lucky enough to see it. Like the Lion King, it reminded me of the ogriginal film but had it's own adnerable qualitys for the stage production.

Greese the Musical was shown at the Dominion Theatre in London West End from June 2 to October 28, 2023.
