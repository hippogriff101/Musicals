Hamilton

This is my favorite musical, I love it SO much!

I got to go with my dad and some friends to the London West end to see it during my half term break!

Starlight Express

Starlight Express is an amazing musical composed by Andrew Lloyd Webber that came back to the West End in 2024.

It is a incredible musical and the roler scates are soo cool! I was lucky enough to go with school in 2025.

Matilda the Musical

Matilda the Musical is based on the hit book by Roald Dahl. The musical adaptation combines my love for books with theater.

I went with my family and again with school!

It is shown at the Cambridge Theatre in the London West End.

The Lion King

I went to see The Lion King in London West End for my birthday in 2024 and my gosh it was phenomenal. It combines my love & nostalgia of the orginal film with the wonders of the theater world.

The Lion King is shown at the Lyceum Theatre in London West End.

Grease the Musical

Grease the Musical was shown in the London West End for a short duration of time in 2023 and I was lucky enough to see it. Like the Lion King, it reminded me of the original film but had it's own admirable qualities for the stage production.

Grease the Musical was shown at the Dominion Theatre in London West End from June 2 to October 28, 2023.

Six the Musical

I love SIX, it is an amazing musical that tells the story of the wives of King Henry VIII, in incredible pop fashion.

Six the Musical is shown at the Vaudeville Theatre in London West End and I went this October!
